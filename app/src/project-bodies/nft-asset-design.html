A study of NFT display devices, platforms, and visuals. The company planned to launch an NFT display platform that, once linked to a personal wallet, would showcase your NFT collection on the wall.

Beyond the device itself, I created a set of in-house NFT pieces for display. Spanning a 'lively' and a 'classical' series, they push past traditional 2D and animated NFTs: built on baked 3D models looping skeletal animation, so users can rotate and inspect each piece from every angle — works made to be admired and toyed with. The varied dynamic materials use OSL shaders, cloth, vertex distortion, and other techniques. Only still frames are shown below.
