An installation of nature projected onto the wall: you can watch time, weather, voice, and body movement shape the scene in many ways — fluttering butterflies, wildly growing vines, and four distinct times of day.

A later iteration added weather states — clear skies, snow, and rain — and dropped the vines in favor of a more breathable image and a space with a stronger sense of depth.
